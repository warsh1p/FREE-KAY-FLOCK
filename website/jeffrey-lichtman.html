Who is Jeffrey Lichtman?




Why is he representing Kay Flock in his murder case?

Before we get into that, let's get into who exactly Jeffrey Lichtman is.

Jeffrey Lichtman, born June 5, 1965 is a criminal defense lawyer who recieved his education in Emory University, graduating with a bachelor's degree in 1987, following it up by attending Duke University School of Law & graduating in 1990. Ever since then, he's been hired by countless people, including (but not limited to) El Chapo, John Gotti Jr, The Game, Fat Joe, etc.

Jeffrey has been noted as a well prepared man for his vehement cross-examination of the witnesses in court cases, leading to favorable results for the defense, some of which are completely amazing, such as the time where he got the sentence of a man accused of sexual assault vacated. This means that the man who already served 27 years has won his freedom & does not have to return to prison again. Or the time he got a $6,000,000 medical fraud case dismissed, where his client was facing upwards of 25 years in prison. With these accomplishments in mind, it's no mystery as to why he's been awarded wtih a litany of awards, including being listed in the bar register of preeminent lawyers, where only 14,000 laywers out of 1,300,000 are listed. He's also recieved the highest rating from the Martindale-Hubbell Legal Directory, a prestegious law directory, as well as recieving the highest rating from the Avvo Lawyer Directory too. To top it all off, He's been named as a New York City Super Lawyer for being so ahead of the rest in his field of criminal defense.

In fact, he's aware of who Kay Flock is & the situation he faced while allegedly shooting & killing Wasca in self-defense that it's not that far a stretch to think he's defending him at a discounted price.
